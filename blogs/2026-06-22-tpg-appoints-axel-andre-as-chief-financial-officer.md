TPG Inc. has appointed Axel André as Partner and Chief Financial Officer, effective July 27, 2026, replacing Jack Weingart who will focus full-time on leading the firm's Global Wealth Solutions business. André brings decades of financial leadership experience from roles at Reinsurance Group of America, AIG, and Goldman Sachs.
