TPG Real Estate has launched Arcura Medical Properties, a new European healthcare real estate platform, following its €400 million acquisition of 30 medical outpatient properties across Germany and the Netherlands. The platform aims to meet growing demand for high-quality clinical infrastructure and pursue growth in key European markets under CEO Simon Betty.
